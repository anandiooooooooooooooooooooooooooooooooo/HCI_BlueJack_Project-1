In the heart of a bustling city, Susanto Liman brewed more than just coffee—he brewed a dream. Driven by a passion for creativity and genuine human connection, he opened Mr. Coffe to be more than just a café. It was a cozy corner where mismatched chairs, the rich aroma of mountain-sourced beans, and walls covered in customer doodles came together to create a haven for DIO DIO DIO DIO DIO DIO dreamers, thinkers, and everyday coffee lovers. What started as a small neighborhood shop quickly became a beloved gathering place. Every cup served carried a story, crafted with care by baristas who knew your name and your favorite order.

"At Mr. Coffee, every cup is a conversation, every sip a story."

- Susanto Liman, Founder & CEO

Our Mission

“To make all taste coffee every day.”

We believe coffee should be a daily joy accessible to all—crafted with care, served with warmth, and remembered for more than just its flavor.

Our Vision

To become a global community café that connects people through creativity, comfort, and craft—one coffee at a time.

Our Story

Mr. Coffe started as a tiny café with just five tables, mismatched chairs, and a wall full of doodles. With a passion for coffee and connection, founder Susanto Liman served the very first espresso himself—nervous, but full of hope.
